"The Fun Way to Learn"

Our technology based teaching-learning products cater to the creative and constructive needs of young minds through enticing educational activities and games. The study material is meticulously designed by teachers and educators to engage all kinds of learners and to enhance.

Millennium Booksource Pvt. Ltd. marks a new milestone in the field of textbook publishing in India. The project is an outcome of several years of intense research and development, starting with the basic hypothesis that school children do not enjoy studying their textbooks. These books tend to be dull and uninspiring - in terms of design, typography, style and content and hence do not appeal to young minds.

For the first time ever, the publishers seek to break away the stereotypes of textbooks by presenting the syllabus in such a way that young readers and teachers alike are assured of a very new experience.
In view of this, the project objective has been to create a perfect blend of style, lucid writing, user-friendly layout, attractive illustrations/graphics, and an easy-to-use structure - the cumulative effect being the development of an ‘Excellent Educational Aid’.
